Defines

#define STD_NO_MONOTONIC_CLOCK
If STD_NO_MONOTONIC_CLOCK is true, then either C2nxt doesn't support a monotonic clock on the being-compiled-for-platform, the being-compiled-for platform itself doesn't, or both, and std_steady_clock and related functions are not available.
